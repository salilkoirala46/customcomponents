Smartprobe

Smartprobe is an innovative food temperature monitoring system designed to ensure food safety by providing real-time temperature data. Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, the system automates temperature tracking for food storage and transportation, reducing the risk of contamination. The platform offers an intuitive interface, alerts for temperature deviations, and detailed analytics for compliance with safety standards.

All Aspects Care is a comprehensive website for an NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme) service provider. The platform is designed to offer accessible information, resources, and support for individuals with disabilities and their families. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the website provides easy navigation, user-friendly interfaces, and clear descriptions of the services offered. The site aims to streamline access to essential services, improve communication, and enhance the overall experience for users seeking NDIS support.
